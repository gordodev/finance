About NYSE Listings

At the New York Stock Exchange, we are driven every day to lead through innovation and are proud to be the exchange of choice for those who embrace new thinking and fresh ideas. As a result, we have welcomed 70% of technology company proceeds since 2014, 70% of SPAC proceeds this year, and 100% of Direct Listings. The NYSE’s unique combination of experience and industry-leading technology allows us to keep our markets moving forever forward.
